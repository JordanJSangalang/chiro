About the Doctor

Hello, I'm Dr. Dawn Sangalang, a dedicated chiropractor with over 30 years of experience. I graduated from Logan College of Chiropractic in 1990 and have been committed to improving my patients' health and wellness ever since. Family is a core part of my life. I have been happily married for over 30 years and am a proud parent of three children.

This experience has deeply influenced my approach to health, emphasizing the importance of maintaining balance and well-being at every stage of life. In my free time, I cherish traveling and exploring new destinations with my family. These experiences enrich my practice, where I aim to provide a nurturing and supportive environment. I look forward to helping you achieve optimal health and a vibrant life.
